"Is there an easy way to calculate ? " I am sure you must have asked this question at least once in your life. Have you ever wondered why some people are so good at number crunching, while others struggle to add even the single digits correctly or without straining their mind.

Before we go any further, let me tell you one very interesting fact about your mind. Human mind is designed to be extremely lazy . This in turn makes it super efficient.

Confused ?? Read on...
Our mind always seeks to get maximum work done using minimum effort.

This makes it super productive . We want that at a click of a button ( that too if absolutely unavoidable ) everything should be done. Our clothes and dishes washed, our food cooked, our rooms cleaned, our algebra homework done.
I am sure you could have solved the question above using the harder way (read: inefficient way) . But you chose not to as your mind knew that it was not the easy (read: efficient) way of doing it.
The person who is good at Math knows by default how to use this quality of mind to his advantage. That is to get maximum work done using bare minimum effort.
